Nettles Tire and Auto Svc. Center, LLC

November 18, 2024

Because the business was located in a rural area, the owners were inundated with calls from customers inquiring how to get there. The main objectives of the site were to 1) give customers an overview of services offered, and 2) to provide easy-to-find directions to get there.

I coded the website, wrote the copy, and used SEO to get the page to the top of Google search results.
